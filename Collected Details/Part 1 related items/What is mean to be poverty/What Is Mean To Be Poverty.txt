Simply We can define Poverty is about not having access to basic infrastructures of people.

According to Wikipedia,

”Infrastructure is the set of fundamental facilities and systems that support the sustainable functionality of households and firms.”

https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Infrastructure

Poverty may be a lack of money to fulfil their needs,
but Poverty is about more than a lack of money.


According to the World Bank Organization, They define poverty as follows, 

“Poverty is hunger. Poverty is lack of shelter. Poverty is being sick and not being able to see a doctor. Poverty is not having access to school and not knowing how to read. Poverty is not having a job, is fear for the future, living one day at a time.”

"What Is Poverty?" Government of New Brunswick, Canada. N.p., n.d. Web. 19 Apr. 2015. <http://www2.gnb.ca/content/gnb/en/departments/esic/overview/content/what_is_poverty.html>.
